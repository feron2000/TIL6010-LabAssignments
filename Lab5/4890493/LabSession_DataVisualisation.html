Q: Is there a relation between the total bill and the amount of tip given?

In [21]:
g = sns.FacetGrid(tips, col="smoker", row='time', hue='sex')
g.map(sns.scatterplot,'total_bill', "tip").add_legend()

plt.show()

A: Looking at the chart above you can see a relation beteen total bill and the amount of tips in the following three scenarios:

  • time = lunch and smoker = Yes
  • time = lunch and smoker = No
  • time = lunch and smoker = Yes

A linear regression line could be made to prove this theorem with hard numbers. You can also see that gender does not matter for this trend. However, you can see a difference in the amount of tip given by men and the total bill that is paid.

At last, it can be seen that there is no clear relation between total bill and the amount of tips for smokers that are paying during dinner.

For more precise and thrustworthy results it is recommended to extend the dataframe with more measurements.
